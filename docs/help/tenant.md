# Tenant Guide

## Overview

This guide explains how tenants use the portal to view invoices, make payments, submit maintenance requests, and access documents.

## Quick Links

- Tenant portal home: `/tenant`
- Payments & invoices: `/tenant/finances`
- Maintenance: `/tenant/maintenance`
- Documents: `/tenant/documents`
- Account settings: `/tenant/settings`

## Sign Up & Account Verification

Where: Invite link or `/auth/signup`

Steps:

1. Open the invite link received from your property manager.
2. Fill in name, email, and password.
3. Click the verification link sent to your email.
4. Log in to access the tenant portal.

If you do not receive a verification email, check spam or request a new invite from your manager.

Image placeholder: `/public/help-assets/placeholder-200x120.svg`

## View & Pay Invoices

Where: `/tenant/finances`

Steps:

1. Open **Invoices** to see outstanding and past invoices.
2. Click an invoice to view details and line items.
3. Click **Pay** and choose a payment method.
4. Confirm the amount and complete payment.
5. A receipt will be emailed and available in Documents.

Notes:

- Displayed currency is set by the property manager in Finance settings.
- If amounts look incorrect, contact your property manager.

Image placeholder: `/public/help-assets/placeholder-200x120.svg`

## Add a Payment Method

Where: `/tenant/settings` → Payments

Steps:

1. Click **Add Payment Method**.
2. Enter card or bank details as prompted.
3. Confirm micro-deposit if required.
4. The method is saved for future payments.

Image placeholder: `/public/help-assets/placeholder-200x120.svg`

## Submit a Maintenance Request

Where: `/tenant/maintenance` → New Request

Steps:

1. Click **New Request**.
2. Select property and unit.
3. Describe the issue, set urgency, and attach photos.
4. Submit and await updates via notifications.

Image placeholder: `/public/help-assets/placeholder-200x120.svg`

## Access Documents

Where: `/tenant/documents`

Steps:

1. Browse the documents list for leases, receipts, and notices.
2. Click to preview or download.
3. Sign documents if e-signature is enabled.

Image placeholder: `/public/help-assets/placeholder-200x120.svg`

## Troubleshooting — Common Issues

- Can't log in: use Forgot Password or request a new invite from the manager.
- Payment declined: check card details and bank balance; try another method.
- Missing invoice: confirm your account is linked to the correct unit.
